Website Design


Creating the look & feel of website




My approach to website design is to create a website that strengthens company's brand while ensuring ease of use and simplicity for audience. Simplicity is one of the golden rules of website design. The audience should have an enjoyable, positive experience when using website. Whether their objective is reading content, watching a video or enrolling in a course, every action should be clear and concise throughout the website.


Responsive Website Design



I build Responsive websites for an optimal user experience. Having a responsive layout means that website fluidly resizes for optimal viewing regardless of the screen size or device (desktop computer, laptop, tablet or smart phone). This ensures ease of use and a better audience experience without the need for panning, scrolling or resizing. Audience is much more likely to enjoy viewing website on a mobile device without needing to zoom in to read the text or to find the navigation menu.
